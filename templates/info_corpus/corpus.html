Corpus Details

The current corpus is somewhat limited, but I hope to continue OCRing and adding more texts. The initial corpus consisted of 24 texts and four different authors and was meant to fill a gap in current textual corpora in which Sufi and other texts are significantly underrepresented.

In addition, and perhaps more importantly, I am encoding these texts according to the Open Islamicate Text Initiative's (OpenITI) mARkdown schema. Encoding these texts renders them "machine actionable" or, in other words, allows scholars to perform a variety of textual analyses. Once encoded, links to the files can be found while browsing the corpus.

The initial corpus was OCR'd using ABBYY FineReader, but moving forward this project will use eScriptorium, a digital palaeography framework. It uses the kraken OCR engine, and for our project we use a kraken OCR model developped by the OpenITI team.
