A Corpus of [Digitally] Neglected Texts

The nuṣūṣ project initially began as a way to make early Sufi and Sufi-adjacent texts available to scholars to read and use for textual analysis. There are a variety of other digitized textual corpora for Islamicate sources, but Sufi texts are severely underrepresented. However, it is clear that Sufism is only one of many other neglected areas in these corpora. Nuṣūṣ therefore aims to include such genres as kalām and falsafa alongside the initial corpus Sufi works.

The texts currently in the nuṣūṣ corpus have been encoded according to the Open Islamicate Text Initiative's (OpenITI) mARkdown schema and, at some point in the near future, will be made available to download for scholars to use for computational analysis. For more information on the corpus, click here.

This is an open source project and the github repo can be found here and in the footer.

We are looking for contributors, so if you are interested in joining this project or learning more, please contact us!
