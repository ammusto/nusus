A Corpus of Digitally Neglected Texts

Nuṣūṣ is a corpus of digitized Arabic texts designed to fill gaps in extant digital corpora. Originally a collection of early-Sufi and Sufi-adjacent texts, Nuṣūṣ has since expanded to include early works on kalām and falsafa. Through this website, users can: browse text metadata, including author biographies; read these works online; and, most importantly, search the contents of texts in the corpus. Soon, the digitized verions of these texts will be made available to download for individuals to use for computational textual analysis or other interests.

For more information on the corpus and its content, click here.

We are looking for contributors, so if you are interested in joining this project or learning more, please contact us!
